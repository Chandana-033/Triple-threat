Popular Government Schemes

Explore various government initiatives designed to support farmers and boost agricultural productivity.

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Income support of ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments to all land holding farmer families.

Learn More →

Soil Health Card Scheme

Provides farmers with soil health cards containing crop-wise recommendations of nutrients/fertilizers.

Learn More →

PM Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance scheme to provide financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage.
